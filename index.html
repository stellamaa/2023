2023

A longing. For home. That I once found in you

Now in another life. Still the same heart beats. The one we found in the other. When the mirror was open.

That which words can’t speak without staining the meaning of. The core. The being of time, of space, the spark that unites us and keeps us eternal.

Soon enough, well dance again. Into the other.

And then my vocal cords were cut
One string at a time
Now I'm only eyes and ears
Forcing me into observance
What a space to be in

A relief for the mind
A struggle too
So used to blurting out everything that passes through my mind
But as the fly
Eyes more wide
When I'm let off the post of the whistle blower
I am the listener

I am the eye of life
